About Us

Prepease started with a simple idea: your kitchen deserves sauces and spreads that taste unforgettable, but don’t demand hours of prep. We combine heritage spices and modern techniques to bring you products that are bold, balanced, and reliable.

Operating from Nagpur, India, we partner with local farmers for fresh ingredients, use minimal processing, and test every batch for consistency. We believe in sustainability, clean food, and culinary delight.
